Hurricane Melissa was one of the strongest hurricanes on record and its impact catastrophic. It passed over Jamaica on October 28th.

Below is the view of Jamaica before and after the hurricane's landfall. We show two RTC-S1 images and well as the DIST-ALERT-HLS.
